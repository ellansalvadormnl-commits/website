A CASUAL T SHIRT

A casual T-shirt is a comfortable, informal top designed for everyday wear. It is one of the most widely worn clothing items in the world because of its simplicity, versatility, and comfort.

Design and Structure of a T-Shirt

The design and structure of a T-shirt focus on simplicity, comfort, and functionality. Although it looks basic, several construction elements work together to create its shape, fit, and durability.

Basic Shape and Silhouette

The basic shape and silhouette of a T-shirt describe its overall outline, structure, and the way it falls on the body. Although a T-shirt appears simple, its proportions and construction strongly influence comfort, appearance, and style. The silhouette determines whether the shirt looks fitted, relaxed, modern, classic, or oversized.
